This morning, I ran the Four on the Fourth Race in Carrboro, North Carolina. This race was a part of le Tour de Carrboro series.

I placed second overall in the four-mile race, with a time of 20:44. I had a faster time and the win in mind, but I had fun! If you are in the area, check out their racing series!
